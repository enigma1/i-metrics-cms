The newletters plugin generates email templates sending emails to your subscribers

The newsletters list page contains all newsletters created. Each time a newsletter is sent, the associated newsletter row displays the total numbers the email is sent out.

The plugin includes feedback control so for each customer and each time a newsletter is sent out the associated entry displays a success percentage the newsletter was viewed.

Newsletters can be copied or edited or deleted by using the associated icon of an entry or selecting a newsletter and hit the appropriate button. Newsletter templates can also be downloaded and edited externally.

To send a newsletter, select the newsletter and click the send button. Newsletters will be send to subscribed customers only. The sender can be configured once the send button is clicked. The sener list is retrieved from the existing helpdesk departments.
