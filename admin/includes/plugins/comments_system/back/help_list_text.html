Text Pages to Comments Assignment

The list of entries here shows the text pages associated with the comments sytem. Marked entries indicate the text pages will include or exclude a comments form on the web-front subject to the plugin configuration options.

When the plugin operates in inclusive mode the text pages listed here will include a comments form on the web-front. In exclusive mode, pages listed here will not include a comments form.

To confirm a new assignment tick the checboxes first then hit the update button.

To manage the posted comments use the main comments page. To go back to the main comments page hit the back button.
